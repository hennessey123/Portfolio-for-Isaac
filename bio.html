My Story

Since first recieving a Nintendo DS from my parents when I was six years old, I became obsessed with the games I was able to play, and my parents began to wonder if they had made a bad decision.

Graduating highschool in 2022 from a liberal arts school, I found myself thinking more about math and science than anything else I was taught. With a very small worldview and a dream, I decided I would study computer science at Samford.

Throughout the course of my study at Samford, I began to see a much bigger world, which initially scared me enough to change my course of study a few times. Eventually however, I found myself back where I started, equipped with a new perspective from other career paths.

Although there are many times I have wanted to quit, I am hopeful God will use the abilities and gifts I have had to learn to his glory.
